commission process

  1. contact me with your idea, genre, references, budget and deadline.
  2. i will confirm whether i can complete the project and provide a final price.
  3. payment is sent before i begin working unless we agree on another arrangement.
  4. i will send a preview or work-in-progress version for feedback.
  5. after revisions are finished, you will receive the final audio files.

terms

commissions include up to two reasonable revisions unless stated otherwise.

major changes requested after the original idea has been approved may cost extra.

commercial use must be discussed before the commission begins.

clients may not claim that they personally produced the music.

i may share finished work in my portfolio unless private work is agreed on beforehand.

refunds are not available after significant work has been completed.
